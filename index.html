Privacy Policy – AffirmGate

Effective Date: Feb 22, 2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AffirmGate does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location data, contacts, messages, or media files.

AffirmGate does not require account creation. All user preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. Accessibility Service Usage

AffirmGate uses Android’s Accessibility Service solely to detect when a selected (blocked) app is opened and display an affirmation screen before allowing access.

AffirmGate does not read personal content, monitor browsing history, record screen activity, or transmit app usage data externally. All accessibility processing occurs entirely on the device.

3. Subscription & Billing

AffirmGate offers optional premium subscriptions through Google Play.

Payments and subscription processing are handled securely by Google Play Billing and RevenueCat.

AffirmGate does not store credit card information or payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

AffirmGate uses:

  • Google Play Billing
  • RevenueCat

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage

All app preferences, selected categories, and blocked app selections are stored locally on your device using Android secure storage.

No user data is transmitted to external servers by AffirmGate.

6. Children’s Privacy

AffirmGate does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected by the effective date.
